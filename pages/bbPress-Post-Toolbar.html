bbPress Post Toolbar

I made this to replace existing functionality on Tartarus for when I upgrade my site to bbPress 2.0. The toolbar shows buttons for some text styling, links, embedding images and YouTube videos. The [video] shortcode enables the embedding of videos (supported providers: Dailymotion, LiveLeak, Megavideo, Metacafe, Vimeo, YouTube) by wrapping the video link with tags, such as:

[youtube]http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RZ-uV72pQKI[/youtube]

Download: http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/bbpress-post-toolbar/
